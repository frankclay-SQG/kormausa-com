Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KORMA-USA collects, uses, shares, and retains personal information when you use our website, request information, register, apply for services, sign a waiver, enroll in programs, or communicate with us.

Important Notice

KORMA-USA does not sell personally identifiable information (PII) or customer data, whether through this website or in any other way.

KORMA-USA may share limited personally identifiable information as reasonably necessary with Korean martial arts governing, certifying, or rank-issuing bodies in connection with rank registration, certification review, promotion processing, lineage verification, and related administrative requirements.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the KORMA-USA website, online forms, application workflows, billing communications, and related digital services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and communication preferences.
  • Program, inquiry, registration, and application details you submit through our forms.
  • Waiver and safety information, including emergency contacts, guardian details, signatures, and consent choices.
  • Address validation data returned by Google services, including standardized address data and place identifiers.
  • Technical and preference data, including cookie-consent choices and locally stored application drafts.

3. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide information about classes, seminars, and services.
  • Create and maintain contact, inquiry, registration, waiver, and application records.
  • Process enrollments, billing communications, and administrative workflows.
  • Validate mailing addresses before accepting address-based submissions.
  • Prevent duplicate records and keep CRM data accurate.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

  • HubSpot for CRM records, inquiry tracking, and application workflows.
  • Google Maps / Google Places / Google Address Validation to search, validate, and standardize mailing addresses.
  • Stripe to create checkout sessions for paid enrollments.
  • Resend for billing email delivery where that workflow is used.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information through this website. We may share information with service providers that help us run the site and support KORMA-USA operations, and when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security.

6. Contact Us

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to info@kormausa.com or masterclay@kormausa.com.
